Leonardo da Vinci

art & design is my passion

Leonardo da Vinci (1452–1519) was an Italian painter, inventor, and polymath widely regarded as one of the greatest artists in history. He created iconic masterpieces such as “Mona Lisa” and “The Last Supper,” known for their detail, emotion, and groundbreaking techniques. Beyond painting, Leonardo studied anatomy, engineering, architecture, and science, filling his notebooks with sketches of flying machines, human anatomy, and innovative inventions. His curiosity, creativity, and visionary ideas made him a symbol of the Renaissance and one of the most influential figures in both art and science.!
